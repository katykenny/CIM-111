ALEX'S ARMY

Supporting Alex Figueroa's Fight Against Ewing Sarcoma

ABOUT ALEX

Alexandra Figueroa is 15 years old and a sophomore at Hendrick Hudson High School. She is a honors student, a three sport athlete- starring on her basketball, soccer, and lacrosse teams- and a beautiful soul. Alex is a loyal friend to so many, an amazing daughter to her parents Jennifer and Rick, and the most important person in her younger sister Madison's life.

HER FIGHT

On April 25, after discovering a mass on her shoulder that seemed to be growing rapidly, Alex was diagnosed with metastatic Ewing's Sarcoma. The cancer has metastasized to her lungs and Alex has been in aggressive chemotherapy and radiation for the past few months. She has been admitted into Columbia Presbyterian for her treatments.
