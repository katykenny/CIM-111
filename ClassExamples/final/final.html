ABOUT

Katy Kenny is a New York City based singer/songwriter. Her style is described as high energy alternative rock, with hints of pop & indie music. Using her powerful mezzo soprano voice and profound lyrics, she engages audiences to listen to her sometimes hilarious, sometimes heartbreaking stories through song. Her influence spans across genres, from artists like Nirvana, Radiohead, Elton John and The Beatles, to modern artists like Paramore, The 1975 and Demi Lovato.
While currently enrolled in the University of Miami's Frost School of Music, she is studying musicianship, artistry development, and entrepreneurship with an emphasis in both music business and songwriting.
