Step 1

  1. Combine 1/2 cup sugar, brown sugar, butter, and peanut butter in bowl.
  2. Beat at medium speed until light and fluffy.
  3. Add egg, vanilla, and salt; continue beating until well mixed.
  4. Add flour and baking soda; beat at low speed, scraping bowl often, until well mixed.
  5. Cover with plastic food wrap; refrigerate 30 minutes.

Step 2

  1. Using a small cookie scoop (1.5 tablespoon size is perfect), or your hands, shape dough into balls a little larger than 1 inch in diameter.

Step 3

  1. Shape dough into 1 1/4-inch balls.
  2. Roll balls in 1/4 cup sugar.
  3. Place 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.

Step 4

  1. Place approximately 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheet.
  2. Bake 9-10 minutes, until lightly golden brown and tops of cookies have a cracked appearance.

Step 5

  1. Press a chocolate kiss into the center of each cookie.
  2. Remove to a cooling rack to continue cooling.
